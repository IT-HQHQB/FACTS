👑 Executive Management

The Executive Management role is the final approval authority in FACTS. Cases arrive after clearing all four field-level reviews (Welfare Review → ZI → KG Review → Operations Lead). The Executive is the person who fills the Cover Letter Form — entering recommended Enayat/Qardan amounts, proposed upliftment plan, non-financial assistance details, welfare department comments, and their digital signature. When the final Executive level approves, the system automatically generates the Cover Letter PDF and advances the case to Manzoori (Sanction). FACTS supports up to 5 executive approval levels.

Login & Landing on the Executive Dashboard

The Executive logs in via browser. The system identifies their executive level (1 through 5) from users.executive_level and loads a dashboard showing only cases currently awaiting their level's approval.

  • Navigate to the FACTS URL → enter Email / Username + Password → click Login
  • System reads users.executive_level to scope the dashboard — only cases where cases.current_executive_level matches this user's level are shown
  • Dashboard stat cards: Cases Awaiting My Approval · Cover Letters Generated This Month · Sent for Rework · Cases at Other Levels
  • A Recent Cases panel shows cases most recently forwarded from the Operations Lead stage, sorted by SLA urgency
  • Bell icon shows unread notifications — cases arriving at the Executive's level trigger an automatic in-app notification

04
Reviewing Status History, Comments & Counseling Form

Before filling the Cover Letter Form, the Executive reads the full case history, all prior reviewer comments, and the Counseling Form — especially the financial sections. This review directly informs the recommended and approved amounts they will enter in the Cover Letter Form.

Status History & Comments
  • Click Status History → full audit trail: Case Created → Draft → Counseling Submitted → Welfare Review → ZI Approval → KG Review → Operations Lead → Executive Approval Level 1 (and higher if applicable)
  • Click Comments → read all notes: DCM, Welfare, ZI, KG, Operations Lead, and any prior executive level comments (if Level 2+)
  • If at Level 2 or higher, read all prior executive level comments and entries carefully before filling the form — understand what amounts and reasoning previous levels used
Counseling Form — Executive Focus Areas
Section Executive Focus — Informs Cover Letter Form Entries
Section 2 — Family Details Total family income (income_total_monthly), monthly surplus/deficit, declared assets and liabilities — the financial foundation for setting approved Enayat and Qardan amounts
Section 4 — Financial Assistance Requested Enayat and Qardan amounts; 5-year repayment schedule — compare against what the Executive will enter as recommended and approved figures
Section 5 — Economic Growth 5-year Revenue, Expenses, Profit, and Cash Surplus projections — Year 1 Cash Surplus must support the proposed Qardan monthly repayment
Section 6 — Action Plan & Mentors Specific milestones and mentor qualifications — directly informs the Proposed Upliftment Plan the Executive writes in the Cover Letter Form
Section 7 — Declaration All three signatures (Applicant, Counselor, TR Committee) present with valid sequential dates
Attachments Check
  • Click Attachments → confirm mandatory documents: Business photograph Uploaded, Quotation for Qardan end-use Uploaded, Product brochure Uploaded
  • Verify the Quotation amount matches the Qardan being considered — a mismatch at this stage after all field reviews is a material concern
  • If optional financial documents (ITR, Financial Statements) are present, cross-check declared income against Section 2 figures

Filling Recommended & Approved Amounts, Upliftment Plan & Comments
⭐ These fields are filled only by the Executive — unique to Executive Management

The Executive now fills the editable fields that form the official financial recommendation and narrative. These are the heart of the Cover Letter Form — they will appear on the final Cover Letter PDF generated after approval.

1. Recommended Financial Assistance Executive enters
recommended_enayat · recommended_qardan · recommended_total — cover_letter_forms table
Recommended Enayat (₹)
Enter amount
Recommended Qardan (₹)
Enter amount
Recommended Total (₹)
Auto-calculated

The Executive's recommended amounts — may match or differ from the applicant's requested amounts. These represent the Executive's independent assessment based on the case review.

2. Final Approved Amounts — The Sanctioned Figures Final Executive Level enters
approved_enayat · approved_qardan · approved_qh_months — cover_letter_forms table
Approved Enayat (₹)
Enter final approved amount
Approved Qardan (₹)
Enter final approved amount
Approved QH Months
Number of repayment months

The binding sanctioned amounts that flow into Manzoori and financial disbursement. Entered by the final (highest) executive level. These become the official approved figures on the Cover Letter PDF.

3. Proposed Upliftment Plan Executive enters
proposed_upliftment_plan — cover_letter_forms table
Proposed Upliftment Plan — long-form text, informed by Section 6 Action Plan in the Counseling Form
Write a specific, time-bound plan: include milestones, expected income growth targets aligned with 5-year projections, support mechanisms per year, and monitoring checkpoints. Reference the action_plan timeline periods (upto_1st_year_end, 2nd_and_3rd_year, 4th_and_5th_year) from the Counseling Form's financial_assistance_action_plan data.
4. Non-Financial Assistance Executive enters
non_financial_assistance — cover_letter_forms table
Types of non-financial support — reference support_needed JSON in financial_assistance table
E.g., Mentorship (mentor name from Section 6 financial_assistance_mentors), Industry Knowledge training, Sourcing assistance, Sales & Market support, Internship placement, Bookkeeping support, Handholding. Match what the applicant selected in Section 4.
5. Welfare Department Comments Executive enters
welfare_department_comments — cover_letter_forms table
Official executive-level comments — appears on the final Cover Letter PDF
E.g., "After thorough review of the full case file, I recommend approving Enayat ₹50,000 and Qardan ₹1,50,000. The applicant's financial position, business viability, and repayment capacity support this assistance. The upliftment plan with quarterly monitoring benchmarks is in place. Approval subject to fund availability."
Recommended vs. Approved Amounts — Important DistinctionThe recommended amounts are the Executive's assessment. The approved amounts (approved_enayat, approved_qardan, approved_qh_months) are the final binding figures for Manzoori and disbursement. These are entered by the final/highest executive level and become the official sanctioned amounts on the Cover Letter PDF.

Signing the Cover Letter Form & Submitting It
⭐ Required before the Approve button is enabled

After filling all required fields, the Executive adds their digital signature to the Cover Letter Form and clicks Submit. Submission permanently locks the form and enables the Approve button in the workflow action panel.

Submitting the Form
  • Review all filled fields one final time — once submitted, all fields lock permanently
  • Click Submit Cover Letter Form → confirmation dialog: "Submit this Cover Letter Form? All fields will be locked and your signature will be recorded." → click Confirm
  • System calls PUT /api/cover-letter-forms/:formId/submit → records submitted_by = Executive user ID, submitted_at = timestamp, sets is_complete = 1
  • The Cover Letter Form tab shows a Submitted banner — all fields are now read-only
  • The Approve button in the workflow action panel becomes active
Form Submission ≠ Case ApprovalSubmitting the Cover Letter Form only locks the data and records the signature. The case does not advance yet. The Executive must click Approve in the workflow action panel to formally advance the case — and at the final level, that approval triggers Cover Letter PDF generation.
Approve Button Disabled Until Form Is Submitted (is_complete = 1)The system enforces a hard dependency. If the Executive clicks Approve before submitting the form, the button is greyed out with tooltip: "Please complete and submit the Cover Letter Form first." Rework does not require form submission, but does require a comment.

08
Adding Comments & Making the Decision

With the Cover Letter Form submitted, the Executive posts a comment and uses the action panel to approve, rework, or reject the case.

  • Click Comments tab → read all prior notes → scroll to bottom → type comment → click Post Comment
  • System calls POST /api/cases/:caseId/comments with role_name = "Executive Management", executive_level = this user's level
  • Summarise the reasoning for the recommended and approved amounts; note any conditions attached to the approval
✅ Approve

Cover Letter Form submitted. Calls PUT /api/cases/:caseId/executive-approve. System checks cases.current_executive_level — if intermediate, advances to next level. If final level, triggers Cover Letter PDF generation and advances to Manzoori.

🔁 Request Rework

Issues found. Calls PUT /api/cases/:caseId/executive-rework → case status → Rework Required → DCM notified. After correction, the full field review chain restarts and the case returns to Executive Level 1 before working back up.

Scenario Immediate Action Case Status After Notified
Intermediate Level Approves current_executive_level incremented; status_history updated Executive Approval (Level N+1) Next Level Executive Approvers
Final Level Approves Cover Letter PDF auto-generated; approved amounts sanctioned; case advances Manzoori (Sanction) Finance team, DCM, Admin
Any Level Reworks Case → Rework Required; restarts from Welfare Review after DCM correction Rework Required DCM
09
What Happens After — Cover Letter PDF Generation & Manzoori

When the final executive level approves the case, FACTS immediately and automatically generates the Cover Letter PDF from the completed Cover Letter Form data and advances the case to Manzoori.

⚡
Final Executive clicks Approve → System auto-triggers: (1) Cover Letter PDF compiled from all Cover Letter Form data → (2) Cover Letter tab activates on the case — downloadable by all parties → (3) approved_enayat, approved_qardan, approved_qh_months become the official sanctioned figures → (4) Case status advances to Manzoori (Sanction) → (5) Finance team and DCM notified.
What the Generated Cover Letter PDF Contains
